# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating and fostering a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free community in the Healthcare AI project. We value the participation of everyone and want to ensure that every contributor has a positive and respectful experience.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior We Encourage

- **Respect**: Treat all community members with courtesy and respect
- **Inclusivity**: Welcome people of all backgrounds and experiences
- **Constructive Communication**: Provide and accept feedback constructively
- **Collaboration**: Work together toward common goals
- **Empathy**: Show understanding and support for others
- **Focus**: Keep discussions relevant and professional
- **Patience**: Be patient and understanding with new contributors
- **Professionalism**: Maintain professional language and conduct

### Unacceptable Behavior

We do not tolerate:

- **Harassment**: Including but not limited to offensive comments related to gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, mental illness, neuro(a)typicality, physical appearance, body size, age, race, ethnicity, nationality, language, or religion
- **Bullying & Intimidation**: Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or harassment
- **Unwelcome Sexual Attention**: Sexual comments or advances of any kind
- **Violence & Threats**: Threats of violence, violent language, or violent behavior
- **Trolling & Disruption**: Deliberate disruption of conversations or communities
- **Privacy Violations**: Publishing private information without consent (doxxing)
- **Plagiarism**: Claiming credit for others' work
- **Spam**: Unsolicited promotional content or abuse of community resources
- **Discrimination**: Treating individuals unfairly based on protected characteristics

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All spaces in the Healthcare AI project (GitHub, discussions, issues, PRs)
- Project-related communications
- Project-sponsored events
- Official project accounts and channels
- When representing the project in public spaces

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

1. **GitHub Reports**: Use the report feature on GitHub
2. **Direct Contact**: Email conduct@healthcare-ai.com with:
   - Description of the violation
   - Who was involved
   - When and where it occurred
   - Any relevant context or evidence
3. **Confidentiality**: All reports are treated confidentially

### Response Process

1. **Review**: Maintainers review all reports
2. **Investigation**: We investigate thoroughly and fairly
3. **Response**: We take appropriate action within 7 days
4. **Appeal**: Appeals can be made within 14 days
5. **Resolution**: We communicate our decision to involved parties

### Consequences

Violations may result in:

- **Warning**: First-time minor violations receive a warning
- **Temporary Ban**: 30-day community ban for repeated violations
- **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from the project
- **Legal Action**: If illegal activity is involved, we may pursue legal remedies

The severity and type of response depends on:
- The nature and severity of the violation
- History of previous violations
- Whether it was intentional or accidental
- Whether the person shows remorse

## Specific Guidelines

### For Issue & PR Discussions

- Keep discussions relevant to the issue/PR
- Criticize ideas, not people
- Respond to disagreements with facts and logic
- Accept when someone is right; move forward
- Don't derail conversations off-topic

### For Project Leadership

- Lead by example
- Address violations promptly
- Be transparent about enforcement
- Protect reporter confidentiality
- Recuse yourself from decisions affecting you personally
- Document all reports and decisions

### For New Contributors

- Be patient with questions
- Provide constructive feedback
- Help new contributors learn
- Celebrate contributions and progress
- Include everyone in discussions

## Reporting Process Detail

### What to Include

When reporting, please include:

1. **Your Information**
   - GitHub username
   - Email address
   - Your role (contributor, maintainer, etc.)

2. **Incident Details**
   - Who was involved?
   - What happened?
   - When and where?
   - Links to relevant messages/content

3. **Context**
   - Why do you consider it a violation?
   - How did this affect you?
   - Any previous incidents?

4. **Desired Outcome**
   - What resolution are you seeking?
   - What would make things right?

### Timeline

- **24 hours**: Acknowledge receipt of report
- **7 days**: Initial assessment and communication
- **14 days**: Full investigation and decision
- **21 days**: Resolution and follow-up

## Confidentiality

- Reporter identities are protected
- Details are shared only with maintainers who need to know
- No public disclosure without reporter's consent
- Except where legally required or for ongoing safety concerns

## Appeals Process

If you believe a decision was unjust:

1. Email appeals@healthcare-ai.com within 14 days
2. Provide new information or context
3. Explain why you disagree with the decision
4. A different maintainer reviews the appeal
5. Final decision made within 7 days
